## Podcast Interviews

### Qiological

**Episode 340 — Alchemy, Magic, and Channel Personalities.** A conversation with Michael Max on the inner work of acupuncture, working with channel personalities, and where classical Chinese medicine touches the older ritual traditions that preceded it.  
[Listen](https://qiological.com/podcast/alchemy-magic-and-channel-personalities-zachary-lui/)

### Drunken Acupuncture

A series of conversations with Nick Duchnowski on clinical TCM practice. Most recent first.

**Session 22 — Building Rapport with Patients.** On the craft of patient connection, the ethics of influence, and building trust without compromising clinical boundaries.  
[Listen](https://podcast.tcmstudy.net/podcast/building-rapport-with-patients)

**Session 19 — Artificial Intelligence in TCM.** On using AI tools to enhance acupuncture practice, where the technology helps, where it does not, and what stays human.  
[Listen](https://podcast.tcmstudy.net/podcast/ai-in-tcm)

**Session 17 — Four-Point Needle Technique.** On Korean Four-Point Needle Technique and its use in the clinic.  
[Listen](https://podcast.tcmstudy.net/podcast/four-point-needle-technique)

**Session 16 — Protect Yourself from Your Patients.** On emotional residue, compassion fatigue, and practical protection practices for bodyworkers and acupuncturists.  
[Listen](https://podcast.tcmstudy.net/podcast/protect-yourself)

**Session 15 — Medical Qigong.** On the benefits of a personal qigong practice, prescribing qigong to patients, and using medical qigong as a method of treatment.  
[Listen](https://podcast.tcmstudy.net/podcast/medical-qi-gong)

**Session 13 — Is Community Acupuncture Real Acupuncture?** On the community acupuncture model, accessibility, and what it means for the future of the profession.  
[Listen](https://podcast.tcmstudy.net/podcast/community-acupuncture)
